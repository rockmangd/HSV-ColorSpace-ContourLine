Tips

  • Link to specific colors directly by adding to the URL a pound sign ("#") followed by the color code in any format.
  • Use the mouse wheel to input text field values while hovering over them or the hue while hovering over the saturation-value palette or hue slider.
  • Store multiple colors at once by clicking the "Save" button in the lower left corner. These will remain saved until you click the "Clear" button next to it, even if you leave the page.
  • After you find a fitting color, you can export it as a hexadecimal, RGB, HSV, or HSL color code.
